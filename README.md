## What Is a MilliPrime?

A MilliPrime is an indy founder with the leverage and systems power of a major corporation. They build systems — not companies. They spin them up, govern them, evolve them, and when their work is done, they retire them cleanly and move on.

**They are driven by rage at injustice and ferocity in execution.**

This isn't a movement we're trying to create. It's a phenomenon we're naming.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Design separates winners.** In abundance, design is the scarce resource.
2. **God Mode is moral when automated.** Orchestrate systems, not humans.
3. **Build vs. Buy flips.** When v1 takes hours, always build.
4. **The unit of competition is the loop.** Tightest feedback loop wins.
5. **Co-ops beat empires when creation is cheap.**
6. **Governance is the new moat.**
7. **Ecosystems form around system owners, not companies.**
8. **Exit becomes a feature.** Clean exit is hygiene, not failure.
9. **The virtue play is economically viable now.**
10. **Shared primitives create leverage.** That's Greenspaces.
